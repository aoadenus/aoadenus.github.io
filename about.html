My Story

Building at the

I'm a Computer Information Systems student at the University of Houston with an Associate of Arts in Business from Houston City College. I've always been drawn to the space where technology and business logic meet — where a well-designed system can change how an entire organization operates.

At Accellux Solutions, I got a front-row seat to how enterprise systems work at scale — supporting SAP S/4HANA FICO implementation for clients like Halliburton and Siemens, contributing to financial data extraction, report validation, and system testing under SAP Activate methodology.

My hospitality roles weren't just jobs — they sharpened my instincts around operational systems, process accuracy, and data-driven decision making from the ground floor up.

Long-term goal

"I want to design and improve systems that help organizations run smarter, make better decisions, and create meaningful impact."
